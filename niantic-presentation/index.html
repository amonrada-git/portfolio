03

Stream
Gaming.

Watch the makers play.
Support what you love.

Stream Gaming · On the Schedule

Streamers, games,
and the music behind them.

Every scheduled gaming stream tells you who's playing, which game, and the soundtrack underneath.

Why it matters

This is what "music stays primary" means. Gaming brings in a new audience — but the watch session quietly fills your music history. The path leads back into Apple Music's core product, not away from it.

Stream Gaming · Caster & Donate

Know the caster,
not just the game.

Each gaming stream is anchored to its caster — bio, past streams, the music they champion. Following them means their next session lands on your home.

  • CASTER

    Profile in-stream

    Tap the creator pill to see who's behind the controller — bio, past streams, related artists. The maker becomes part of the show.

  • DONATE

    Tip the streamer · out of scope

    Viewers tip via Apple Pay ($1–$20+). Notifications float on stream and feed a Top Supporter leaderboard. Streamer keeps the bulk; Apple takes a small fee.

The Path Forward

Before

Radio

A station you tune to.

Now

Live

A space you tune in to.

Renaming an existing tab feels bold — but every existing Radio function still lives inside Live. All existing radio channels are preserved. They're just live broadcasts among other live broadcasts now.

The Live tab

One tab, four content modes.

  • 01

    Stations

    Evergreen audio. Apple Music 1, Hits, Country — and a new Play station for game soundtracks.

  • 02

    Live Now

    The carousel that mixes concerts, events, gaming streams, and music shows. Same card anatomy across all four.

  • 03

    Listening party

    Where the social-but-private layer lives. Show the count, hide the names.

  • 04

    On the Schedule

    The queue. Scheduled broadcasts wait here until the Going Live transition fires.

Why each section earns its place

Four roles, no overlap.

Stations

Always available. The Radio tab's familiar set, kept intact. Removing it would break a habit millions already have.

Live Now

Time-bound urgency. A surface that says "right now" — the only place where missing it means missing it.

Listening party

Social without social. Connection that doesn't require trust, friend lists, or chat moderation.

On the Schedule

Anticipation. Where Going Live transitions live before they fire — turning the wait into part of the experience.
